Hello everyone, my name is Mame. After years in the U.S. military, I have dedicated the past several years to advancing my education and skills in technology and skilled trades, with a particular focus on HVAC systems and building maintenance. My early experience in the manufacturing industry as an automation technician gave me a strong foundation in industrial control systems and process optimization.
Through my studies at Fayetteville Technical Community College, I developed a solid understanding of air conditioning, heating, and refrigeration systems, as well as preventive maintenance and energy efficiency practices. I enjoy combining my technical background with hands-on HVAC work, focusing on systems that improve comfort, performance, and sustainability in residential and commercial environments.
Today, I focus on expanding my technical expertise across multiple disciplines, particularly in building systems, HVAC technology, and smart maintenance solutions. My passion lies in integrating technology with skilled trades to create efficient, sustainable, and comfortable living environments. I take pride in delivering quality workmanship and ensuring every system operates at its best.
This website is simple, ad-free, and dedicated to showcasing my professional journey. On the Lab page, you’ll find updates about my ongoing projects, training, and practical work in HVAC installation, maintenance, and diagnostics. I regularly share my hands-on experiences and the progress I make as I continue developing my expertise in air conditioning and refrigeration technology.
Thank you very much for visiting.
