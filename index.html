Hello, my name is Mame. I’m a tech-savvy Maintenance and HVAC Technician with a background in industrial automation, telecommunications, and computer technology. I also recently earned a Basic Air Conditioning, Heating, and Refrigeration Certificate and am currently pursuing an Associate Degree in HVAC Technology at Fayetteville Technical Community College.

I’m passionate about integrating modern technology into traditional skilled trades — leveraging tools like IoT devices, digital diagnostics, and work order management systems to improve efficiency, performance, and safety. My goal is to bring smart, reliable maintenance solutions to every environment I serve.
